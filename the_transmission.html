In His Own Words

On June 24, 2025, Renan dictated his autobiography to ChatGPT. This is a man from rural Pernambuco talking to an AI, telling his life for the first time. The conversation was titled “Autobiografia de Renan.”

He was raised by his grandmother in rural Pernambuco. Born August 12, 1991 in São Joaquim do Monte, a difficult birth that nearly killed his mother. When his grandmother died, something broke. His brother Diego — who he’d raised, who he’d protected — told him in 2022 during a devastating fight: “nem no meu velório iria.” Not even at my funeral would I go. And then Diego went blind.

Cancer came in 2017. Hodgkin lymphoma. Chemotherapy. Remission. Relapse. PET/CT scans in September 2023 and July 2024 tracking progression. Then the terminal diagnosis: the cancer had metastasized to the lungs.
